Services High-Impact Services

High-impact support for calmer digital systems

Technology should make life easier, not noisier. My mindful computing services help people and small teams organize files, notes, accounts, apps, and workflows into systems that feel clearer, safer, and more sustainable.

Personal Knowledge Management

I help set up customized "second brain" systems using tools like Obsidian, Notion, or Apple Notes. By establishing a clear method for capturing, organizing, and retrieving information, we reduce cognitive load and ensure your ideas are always accessible when you need them.

Digital Decluttering

Overwhelmed by messy hard drives, bloated inboxes, and hundreds of open tabs? We'll work together to audit your digital environments, establish sensible folder structures, implement inbox zero routines, and create sustainable habits to keep your digital space calm and focused.

Privacy & Account Safety

Take back control of your online footprint. I provide guided audits to secure your accounts with password managers and two-factor authentication, review app permissions, and adjust privacy settings across your devices to ensure your data remains yours.

Calm Workflows & AI Integration

Learn how to use technology intentionally rather than reactively. We'll design workflows that minimize distractions, set up focused work environments, and explore how to use AI tools practically to automate tedious tasks without adding unnecessary complexity to your life.
